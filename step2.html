Certificate of non-convictions

The service is available to persons permanently residing abroad.

The service is also available on the website https://e.ic.iem.gov.lv/lv/ For that it is necessary to hold a valid e-signature (included in the ID cards issued by Latvia);

To request a Police Clearance Certificate in English language from the Embassy of Latvia, you will have to submit the following:

1.   Application Form;

2.    The copy a personal identity document (signed and with the written statement: “Kopija pareiza”);

3.    State fee payment – a copy of the proof of payment of the state fee to the State Treasury.

State fee:  the certificate is issued within 5 working days - 16.80 EUR, the certificate is issued within 2 working days - 33.60 EUR.

Payment should be settled in credit institution prior to submission of documents:  

Beneficiary:  MI Information Centre, Registration Certificate No.: 90000289913

Beneficiary institution (Bank): the State Treasury

Code: TRELLV22;

Sub-account: LV96TREL2140088026000

Address: Smilsu street 1, Riga, Latvia

4.     Consular fee payment - 40.00 EUR (must be payed at the embassy)

Short Stay (Schengen) Visa

Citizens and residents (who have their residence permit or Long Stay visa issued by the official authorities) of following countries are eligible to apply for a short stay Schengen visa at the Embassy of Latvia in India:

  • Republic of India;
  • Republic of Maldives;
  • Republic of the Union of Myanmar;

Nationals of the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal should apply for a Schengen visa to Latvia at the Embassy of Switzerland in Nepal- https://www.eda.admin.ch/kathmandu.

Nationals of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka should apply for a Schengen visa to Latvia at the Embassy of the Federal Republic of Germany in Sri Lanka - https://colombo.diplo.de/.

Nationals of the People's Republic of Bangladesh should apply for a Schengen visa to Latvia at the Embassy of Sweden in Bangladesh - https://www.swedenabroad.se/en/embassies/bangladesh-dhaka/.
